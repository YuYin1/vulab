Case is a Unitree G1 humanoid robot at VU Lab, used for research in embodied AI, dexterous manipulation, and human-robot interaction.

## Capabilities

* Bipedal locomotion and balance control
* Dexterous whole-body manipulation
* Embodied task execution in human environments
