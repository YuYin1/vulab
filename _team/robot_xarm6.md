xArm-6 is a UFactory 6-DOF robot arm at VU Lab, used for research in dexterous manipulation, grasping, and task-and-motion planning.

## Capabilities

* 6-DOF robotic manipulation
* Precision grasping and assembly
* Task and motion planning
