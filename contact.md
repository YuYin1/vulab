We welcome research collaborations, student inquiries, and applications from people interested in visual understanding, spatial intelligence, embodied AI, and robotics.

## Opening Positions

We are always looking for self-motivated students and researchers who are interested in visual understanding, spatial intelligence, embodied intelligence, and real-world autonomy.

We welcome different types of collaboration, including PhD applicants, MS research students, visiting scholars, interns, postdocs, and research staff.

However, due to a large volume of inquiries, we may not be able to respond to every message.

For all types of positions and inquiries, please contact the lab with the following materials:

* A current CV
* A short statement of research interests
* Links to representative papers, projects, code, videos, or portfolios

Please note that we typically look for applicants who have one or more of the following:

* Strong hands-on experience with real robotic systems, deployed perception pipelines, or large-scale embodied AI projects
* Publications in strong robotics, computer vision, machine learning, or embodied AI venues
* Distinctive strengths such as competition results, open-source systems work, or major engineering contributions

Please highlight your strongest evidence clearly when reaching out.
